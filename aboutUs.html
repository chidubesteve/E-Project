About us

Chic Lighting & Design is a reputable company that has been operating since 1971, providing unique lighting and accessories for homes and businesses. The company specializes in LED lights, lamps, chandeliers, and pendant lighting, offering a range of collections to suit various tastes and preferences.
The collections at Chic Lighting & Design are carefully curated to cater to customers with different styles, ranging from elegant traditional to refined contemporary designs. They offer crystal chandeliers, traditional chandeliers, modern chandeliers, glass pendant lights, industrial and retro pendant lights, metal pendant lights, and many more options.
One of the company's core values is exceptional customer service. They have a team of knowledgeable and experienced professionals who are always available to assist customers with their lighting projects. Whether it is through email or phone call, customers can be assured of getting a prompt and helpful response from a design professional during normal business hours.
In addition to providing quality lighting fixtures, Chic Lighting & Design also places a strong emphasis on staying up to date with the latest lighting trends and technologies. The company's staff regularly undergoes education and training to ensure they are well-equipped to provide customers with the best lighting solutions available.
Overall, Chic Lighting & Design is a reliable and customer-focused company that has made a name for itself in the lighting industry over the years.
